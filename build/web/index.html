Why EduSync? This app was built to bridge the gap between students and tutors by offering a centralized platform for managing consultations, sharing academic resources, and improving student support systems. It encourages collaborative learning and better access to study materials anytime, anywhere.

Empowering Students Through Smart Learning

EduSync is your academic companion – helping students connect with tutors, manage their modules, and access academic content with ease. Whether you're booking a consultation, uploading material, or asking tough questions — EduSync supports your success.
